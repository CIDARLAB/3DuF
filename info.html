3DuF v1.0

3DuF is microfluidic design environment developed by CIDAR. Source code, issue tracking and feature requests are available at Github.
Usage
del
 - Delete Feature 
left click
 - Place Feature 
right click
 - Select Feature 
mouse scroll
 - Zoom 
arrow keys
 - Pan
Developed by: Aaron Heuckroth, Joshua Lippai and Radhakrishna Sanka.
